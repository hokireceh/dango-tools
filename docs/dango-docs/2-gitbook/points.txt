What are Dango points?

Our way of recognizing those who contribute to Dango’s success. They reflect a user’s overall engagement on our exchange.  1m points are distributed each week.

hashtag

How to earn points

Perps traders share 750,000 points each week

Vault depositors share 250,000 points each week

Referrers receive 1 point for every 5 points earned by their direct referrals, and 1 point for every 20 points earned by their indirect referrals (2-tier system)

hashtag

Boosters

Anyone who owns 1 or more Galxe OATs from Dango&#x27;s testnet quests is eligible for a temporary 4-week points boost. To activate the boost, simply link the EVM wallet(s) containing the OAT(s) to your Dango account. Each unique OAT grants a 100% points boost, up to a maximum of 400%.

Leagues

All users are grouped into leagues based on their total points holdings. Players move up and down leagues at the start of each new week when points are distributed. There is no reward for climbing leagues, it’s simply for fun!

Wood - bottom 30% of points holders  Iron - next 25%  Gold - next 18%  Platinum - next 12%  Diamond - next 8%  Master - next 5%  Grandmaster - top 2%
