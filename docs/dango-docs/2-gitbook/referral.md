To access higher commission tiers, users must increase their 30-day referral volume

Referrers can share up to a maximum of 50% of their commission with referees

Referrers receive 1 point for every 5 points earned by their direct referrals, and 1 point for every 20 points earned by their referees’ referees (two-tier)

Dango uses a tier-difference model:

If an upstream referrer has a higher tier than the direct referrer, they earn the difference between their tier and the direct referrer’s tier

If the upstream tier is equal or lower, they earn nothing
